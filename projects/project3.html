Oscars’ objective is to create a website in HTML and CSS for a bar, Sugar Shot. Based on Oscar’s analysis and experience through different bars in New Orleans, his goal is to create a warm and welcoming page with a pop of color for a chilled yet fun night out. Oscar’s intention is to make a simple, direct page, informing customers what the bar has in store. He also took time to make a menu that would help customers find what they would be looking for right away by creating a separate navigation bar. Directing them based on a catalog of different kinds of alcoholic drinks and a dedicated dessert section on the same page.
