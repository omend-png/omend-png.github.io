Portfolio - Jenene

Jenene

Figma

Readymag

Oscar spent over the summer working for Jim Lagger to help out Broadway singer Jenene. His objective is to research how different artists use their websites to first, branch out to larger audiences and clients. Second, inform her established audience on their upcoming shows. Oscar’s solution is to make their home page welcome everyone to her performing from one of her shows. Then he designed a section on the home page to help returning audiences learn more about her upcoming shows, based on their pre-existing extension that would update automatically from their ticketing service. Then he had a section for clients to have references from her past biggest clients, including Britto for creditability.
