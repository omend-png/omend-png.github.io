Portfolio - AMA Announcement Poster

AMA Announcement Poster

Illustrator

InDesign

Oscar is a social media director with a focus on making designs for their social media platforms. Their intention is to help build a brand reignition and make cognitive posts when announcing meetings. Oscar’s direction led to making a straightforward advertisement of their meeting, only including the subject of the meeting, and basic details like the date and location. He was able to conclude a design that best represents the recognitions on the halftones as an element frequently used by the org. Representing their unity.
