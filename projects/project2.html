Covering Boys Love Zine

InDesign

Photoshop

Print

Oscar did a study on the covers of the Boys Love genre since its origins. As well as its impact on the media of the LGBTQIA+ community. His intention is to inform the general audience about the genre and its history. As well as its taboos, making sure he debunks many myths about the LGBTQIA+ community. The zine features cohesive design that would stand out for both Boys Love readers and the many other users.
