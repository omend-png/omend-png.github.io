Portfolio - AMA T-Shirt

AMA T-Shirt

Illustrator

Photoshop

Oscar wanted to make an impression for the American Marketing Association with their Annual T-Shirt Competition. With their theme of the year being Formula 1, Oscar created a collage of elements that represent the theme while reflecting the chapter. The goal here is to make sure the shirt could be used as a casual t-shirt while discreetly promoting the chapter. Resulting in Oscar becoming the victor for the T-Shirt competition, many students still uses it outside of the chapter for casual wear as well, proudly representing the organization and cause.
