Olympics Pictograms

Illustrator

Print

Photoshop

Lightroom

A collaboration with a team to create a concept of an Olympic in Davao City, Philippines. Oscar’s focus is designing pictograms, which would highlight the community that helped build its essence and culture in Davao City. Oscar developed a set of cohesive designs of pictograms that reflect the signage from street vendors in the Philippines. Yet, making sure the pictograms were recognizable to its respected sport assigned with. These designs were used on ticket designs as well as banners.
