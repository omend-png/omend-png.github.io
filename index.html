About Me

Hi! How you've been?

Oscar Mendoza, a graphic designer based in the Midwest. Currently pursuing his bachelor’s at Illinois State University, with a sequence in graphic design and public relations.

While Oscar is building on his foundation of design, he’s currently helping a student-run organization, the American Marketing Association, to build on their brand and visibility in the College of Business space as a social media director. Advocating for brand recognition and storytelling. Oscar has a desire to create innovative designs for layouts in prints and websites.

In his free time, he enjoys listening to music of many different genres, like Latin pop and hard techno. Going to music festivals, as well as going on adventures (running errands) by himself.
